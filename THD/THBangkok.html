Bangkok, Thailand

Bangkok Information
Bangkok, the capital city of Thailand, is a vibrant metropolis known for its rich cultural heritage, bustling street life, and modern amenities.
Bangkok is located in the central part of Thailand, along the Chao Phraya River, near the Gulf of Thailand.
The city has a tropical monsoon climate, characterized by hot weather throughout the year, with a rainy season from May to October and a dry season from November to April.
